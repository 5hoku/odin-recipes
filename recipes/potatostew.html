Potato stew

Stew to rip through the 4th dimension

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes (any kind)
  • Veggies (carrots, celery, onion)
  • Frozen peas
  • Garlic
  • Dijon mustard
  • Soy sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Herbs (Italian seasoning, rosemary, thyme)
  • Low-sodium vegetable broth or water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

  1. Saute' onion and garlic in olive oil for 5 minutes>
  2. Stir in the diced carrots and celery.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Stir in broth, toss to combine and bring to the boil.
  5. Cook gently, and after 25 minutes add the frozen peas.
  6. Let it simmer for 3 minutes until tender, taste, and adjust the seasoning.
  7. Serve with freshly ground black pepper and crusty bread. Enjoy!
